HEALTH PRODUCTS AND SERVICES:

Health Assessment For Doctors: Health Assessment offers Health Care Providers (“HCP”) a platform to set up and operate their online/virtual ‘clinics’ and offer their services to their patients. Health Assessment: Health Assessment (for Patients, “Users”) allows Users to create and maintain an online account with up-to-date medical profiles, create and maintain a Care Team of their doctors and/or HCPs, seek health advice and request consultations with their doctors and/or HCPs for a fee.

FirstStep: FirstStep is a paediatric app for parents to record, maintain and track the health and well-being of their child using features like personalised immunisation schedules, growth charts and developmental milestones covering a baby’s development in four major areas – cognitive, social & emotional, communication, and physical development.
